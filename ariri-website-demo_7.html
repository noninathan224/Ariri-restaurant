Our story

Born from fire, seasoned with love, served with pride.

Ariri means “taste” in Igbo — and that is exactly what we deliver. Our kitchen brings the soul of Nigeria to the heart of Leicester. Every pot is seasoned with tradition, every dish tells a story passed down through generations. From our firewood party jollof to our slow-cooked egusi, we cook with intention. Welcome to Ariri.
